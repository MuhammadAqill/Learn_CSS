TRANSITION Part 2

Syntax:

  • transition: [property] {durasi} [fungsi] [delay];
  • fungsi
      • timing function
    • ease (default)
    • ease-in
    • ease-out
    • ease-in-out
    • linear
    • cubic-bezier(w,x,y,z)

Contoh Pengunaan

ease
ease-in
ease-out
ease-in-out
linear
cubic-bezier
delay 3s
multiple

Penerangan
  • awalnya perlahan, makin lama makin laju
